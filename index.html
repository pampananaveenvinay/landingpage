An e-library, also known as a digital library, is a virtual repository of digital resources accessible through the internet or specialized software. It houses a diverse collection of electronic materials such as e-books, articles, videos, and more, providing users with global access to information, advanced search capabilities, personalization features, and round-the-clock availability. E-libraries serve as essential tools for research, learning, and exploration in a digital age, offering a convenient and comprehensive alternative to traditional brick-and-mortar libraries.
